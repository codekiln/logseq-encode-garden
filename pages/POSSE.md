- # [POSSE - IndieWeb](https://indieweb.org/POSSE)
	- POSSE means "**Publish (on your) Own Site, Syndicate Elsewhere**".
	- In IndieWeb practice, canonical content lives on your own site, with copies syndicated to third-party platforms.
