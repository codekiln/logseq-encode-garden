- # Find a time tracker to migrate off Toggl Track
	- ## Motivation
		- Want to reduce dependence on **Toggl Track** (web + native clients) and move time tracking closer to [[CLI/Craftsmanship]] — scripts, TUIs, and data that lives in repo- or filesystem-friendly forms.
		- [[tmux/Idea/Toggl Track status in status line]] is a reasonable **bridge** while still on Toggl; migration is the separate bet on a replacement product or workflow.
	- ## What “good” might mean (open criteria)
		- Solid start/stop and **running entry** visibility from the terminal (and ideally [[tmux]]-friendly hooks).
		- **Export / ownership** of history (or self-hosted) so the next move is not another lock-in.
		- Enough project/tag/workspace model to replace current Track usage — exact parity TBD.
