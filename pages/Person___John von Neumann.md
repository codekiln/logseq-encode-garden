- # John von Neumann
	- ## About
		- Hungarian-American mathematician, physicist, computer scientist, and engineer (1903–1957). Born **Neumann János Lajos** in Budapest; naturalized U.S. citizen in 1937.
		- Major contributions across mathematics, quantum physics, economics, statistics, and computing—including foundational work on [[Game/Theory]], operator algebras, and the logical structure of modern digital computers; wartime work on the Manhattan Project and postwar defense and atomic-energy advisory roles.
