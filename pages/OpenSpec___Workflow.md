- # OpenSpec Workflows
	- Each "workflow" is a specific prompt that takes a specific action.
	- [[Examples]]
		- `/opsx:new` is `New change` (not default)
		- `/opsx:ff` is `Fast-forward` (not selected by default)
		- `/opsx:continue` is `Continue change`
		- `/opsx:verify` is `Verify change`
		- `/opsx:/archive` is `Archive change`
	- The workflows that are available are configured in [[OpenSpec/config/profile/Step 3 - Configure Workflow]]
