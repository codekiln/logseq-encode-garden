- # Michael Pollan
	- ## About
		- American author and journalist; writes about nature, food, agriculture, and (in later work) mind-altering substances and consciousness.
	- ## Books (selected)
		- [[Book/How to Change Your Mind]] — psychedelic science, therapy, and what it suggests about consciousness and mental health (2018).
		- Earlier well-known works include *The Omnivore's Dilemma* and *In Defense of Food*.
