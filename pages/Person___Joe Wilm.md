- # [Joe Wilm](https://jwilm.io/)
	- Software engineer and original author of [[Alacritty]], the GPU-accelerated terminal emulator written in [[Rust]]
	- Created Alacritty in 2016 with a focus on simplicity and performance; it was among the first terminals to use GPU rendering
	- The project is now maintained by a community of contributors
