- # [Gherkin](https://cucumber.io/docs/gherkin/)
	- A plain-text [[Programming/Language/DSL]] for executable behavior specifications in [[Behavior/Driven/Development]].
	- Feature files use keywords such as `Feature`, `Scenario`, `Given`, `When`, and `Then`, typically stored as `.feature` files and mapped to step-definition code.
	- Originally developed for [[Cucumber]] and used by compatible BDD tooling such as [[behave]].
