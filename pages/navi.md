- # [denisidoro/navi](https://github.com/denisidoro/navi)
	- Interactive **cheatsheet** runner for the terminal: browse **`.cheat`** snippets, fuzzy-find with **fzf** / **skim**, optionally **execute** templated commands, and wire it as a **shell widget** (e.g. Ctrl-style recall).
	- Cheats can live under `~/.local/share/navi/cheats/` and be **imported from git repos** so dotfiles can version shared cheat libraries.
	- Not a spaced-repetition system; structure and “hierarchy” come from how you **organize files, tags, and sections**.
