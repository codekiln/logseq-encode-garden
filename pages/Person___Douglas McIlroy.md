- # Douglas McIlroy
	- ## About
		- Mathematician and engineer at Bell Labs; led Computing Sciences Research Center, invented the Unix shell pipeline, and in 1978 published the four-point Bell System Technical Journal foreword that codified the “characteristic style” of Unix developers. Often quoted for the trio: one thing well, composition, text streams as a universal interface.
