- # Plain Writing Act of 2010
	- U.S. federal law (Public Law 111-274) requiring federal agencies to write public-facing documents in clear, plain language the audience can understand and use the first time they read it.
	- ## Normative links
		- Law: https://www.govinfo.gov/app/details/PLAW-111publ274
		- Federal plain-language guidance: https://www.plainlanguage.gov/
	- ## Scope
		- Covers documents federal agencies issue to the public. The federal plain-language guidelines extend the law into concrete writing practice.
	- ## Maintainer
		- Guidance is maintained by PLAIN (Plain Language Action and Information Network) at plainlanguage.gov.
