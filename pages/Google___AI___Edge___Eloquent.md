- # [Google AI Edge Eloquent](https://ai.google.dev/edge/eloquent)
	- Voice dictation product in the [Google AI Edge](https://ai.google.dev/edge) family: turns spoken drafts into written text, with emphasis on readable output and on-device or hybrid processing depending on platform and mode.
	- Primary reference: [Google AI for Developers — Eloquent](https://ai.google.dev/edge/eloquent).
	- Related open-source and samples from the same initiative live under the [google-ai-edge](https://github.com/google-ai-edge) GitHub organization (the consumer app itself is not necessarily shipped as a single public application repo).
