- [[Person/Harry Brignull]]
	- **Harry Brignull** (Dr. Harry Brignull) is a UX designer and researcher who coined the term **"dark patterns"** in 2010.
	- **Key Contributions:**
		- Created darkpatterns.org (now rebranded as deceptive.design) to catalog and critique deceptive user interface designs
		- Authored the book *Deceptive Patterns*
		- Serves as an expert witness in legal cases involving deceptive design
		- Works with organizations like Spotify, Pearson, HMRC, the Telegraph, and Smart Pension
	- **Research Focus:**
		- Dark patterns (deceptive patterns) in UX design
		- Ethical design practices
		- User manipulation through interface design
