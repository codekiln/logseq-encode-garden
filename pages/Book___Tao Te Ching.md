- # Tao Te Ching
	- ## About
		- Ancient Chinese classic associated with Daoism and one of its foundational texts.
		- Traditionally attributed to [[Person/Lao Tzu]]; authorship and dates of composition are debated.
		- Also known as the Daodejing or Laozi in scholarship and translation history.
