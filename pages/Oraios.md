- # [Oraios AI](https://oraios-ai.de/)
	- AI **consulting and solution-development** partnership (legal form on site: Jain & Panchenko IT-Berater Partnerschaft). Services include AI solution build-out, use-case workshops and assessment, and AI training. The trade name *Oraios* comes from Greek *oraíos* (“beautiful”).
	- Founded in **2024** by Dominik Jain and Michael Panchenko as friends and long-time collaborators; stated goal is an independent venture that delivers genuine value through deep AI expertise and operational efficiency. [^1]
	- ## Software
		- [[SerenaAI]] — MCP-based toolkit for agentic coding; open source under the `oraios` GitHub organization ([repository](https://github.com/oraios/serena)).
