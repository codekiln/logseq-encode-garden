- # [tldr-pages/tldr](https://github.com/tldr-pages/tldr)
	- Collaborative **cheat sheets** for **console commands**: a large corpus of concise, example-driven Markdown pages (the upstream source many **`tldr`** CLI clients and services such as [[cheat.sh]] draw from).
	- Focus is **portable command snippets** and common invocations—not interactive drill, hierarchical TUIs, or private dotfile-specific bindings unless you **maintain your own pages** or **client config**.
