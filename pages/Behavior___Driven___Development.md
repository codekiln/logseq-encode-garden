- # Behavior Driven Development (BDD)
	- A method of writing [[Software]] that usually entails describing the [[Requirement]]s ahead of time.
	- One framework for using BDD is the [[Py/Lib]] [[behave]], which uses a modified form of [[Gherkin]], which is the [[DSL]] for BDD originally written for [[Cucumber]], a cross-language BDD framework.
