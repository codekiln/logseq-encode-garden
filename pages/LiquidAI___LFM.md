- # [Liquid Foundation Models (LFM)](https://liquid.ai)
	- Family of **efficiency-oriented foundation models** (text, vision-language, audio, and related variants) from the company behind liquid neural network research—positioned for **on-device, edge, and low-latency** deployment as well as cloud use.
	- **Developer surface:** fine-tuning and deployment via [LEAP](https://platform.liquid.ai/) and open weights / Hugging Face listings under the `LiquidAI` org (e.g. LFM2 line).
