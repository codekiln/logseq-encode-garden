- # The Source
	- ## About
		- Historical novel by [[Person/James A. Michener]] (1965) surveying Jewish history and the land of Israel from pre-monotheistic times through the modern state, framed by a 1960s archaeological dig.
		- Central device: a fictional Galilee **tell** named **Makor** (Hebrew “source”); excavated layers and artifacts launch chapter-long historical episodes, alternating with the present-day dig narrative.
		- Makor’s name also refers to a freshwater well north of the mound—symbolically tied to spiritual and historical “source” themes in the plot.
