- # [Easy Copy](https://obsidian.md/plugins?id=easy-copy)
	- [[Obsidian]] community plugin that copies a wikilink anchor to the clipboard based on cursor position.
	- Placing the cursor on a heading line and running **Easy Copy: Contextual Copy** copies `[[NoteName#Heading]]` to the clipboard. Works on blocks and paragraphs too.
