- # Flyover reader
	- The human brain is uniquely designed for tasks of navigation and location.
	- Picture a [[Read/Later/App]] that is more like a [[Game/Video]] that lets you pick various reading modes.
		- Similar to any read-it later app, one could import text.
	- The app would enable one to turn the whole text into a [[Game/Video/Platformer]] where one could jump around the paragraphs like Mario
	- It could also turn the whole text into a vast landscape where the words are actually plants, and one could fly over, crop-dusting with a little by-plane
	- It could turn it into a long road, where the words were on the road, and one ran over them
		- This could be especially fun if one was literally on a treadmill
	- The game could include mechanics for commenting that would spatially embed comments
