- # George Nachman
	- Software engineer and creator of [[iTerm2]], the macOS terminal emulator
	- Forked the original iTerm project (by Fabian Kaiser) in 2011 and rewrote it extensively; iTerm2 became the dominant macOS terminal for over a decade
	- Invented the [[iTerm2/Image/Protocol]] for displaying inline images in the terminal, which was subsequently adopted by other terminal emulators including [[WezTerm]]
	- Has worked at Google
