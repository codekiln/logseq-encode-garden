- # [Clayton Christensen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clayton_Christensen)
	- ## About
		- American management scholar (1952–2020) best known for **disruptive innovation** research at Harvard Business School and for helping popularize **[[Job/to be Done]]** language in product and strategy practice.
	- ## Ideas
		- Demand-side framing that treats markets as collections of **progress** attempts—people pulling solutions into their lives to get a job done—rather than as stable segments defined mainly by demographics or product categories.
	- ## Online presence
		- [Christensen Institute](https://www.christenseninstitute.org/)
