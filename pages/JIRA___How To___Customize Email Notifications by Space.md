- # How to Customize Email Notification Preferences by Space in Atlassian Products
	- ## Overview
		- The path `/jira/settings/personal/notifications` is where you can customize email notification preferences by space in Atlassian products
		- This allows you to control which email notifications you receive for different spaces/projects
	- ## Access Path
		- Navigate to: `/jira/settings/personal/notifications`
		- This is the personal settings page for notification preferences
	- ## Use Case
		- Customize email notification preferences
		- Control notifications by space/project
		- Manage personal notification settings across Atlassian products
