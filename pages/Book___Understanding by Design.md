- # Understanding by Design
	- ## About
		- Foundational pedagogy and curriculum-design framework for **backward design**: clarify desired understanding and acceptable evidence first, then plan learning experiences and instruction.
		- Co-authored by [[Person/Grant Wiggins]] and [[Person/Jay McTighe]]; widely used in K–12 and higher education. First edition 1998; expanded second edition 2005 is a common classroom reference.
