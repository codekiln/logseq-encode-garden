- # Build: An Unorthodox Guide to Making Things Worth Making
	- ## About
		- 2022 book by [[Person/Tony Fadell]] on product-building lessons from General Magic, Apple, Nest, and other operating environments.
		- The [[Planet Money]] episode [[Person/Tony Fadell/Pod/26/06/We almost had a smartphone in the 90s. Why did it fail?]] highlights the General Magic thread: a 1990s attempt to build an early smartphone-like product with abundant funding, elite talent, creative freedom, endless iteration, and too little customer pull.
		- The same episode contrasts that environment with Apple's later constraints: tighter deadlines, clearer product focus, and shipping pressure. Snipd excerpt at about 24:01: "Tony gave his team deadlines."
