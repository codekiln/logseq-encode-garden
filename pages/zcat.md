- # [zcat](https://www.gnu.org/software/gzip/manual/gzip.html)
	- **[[Unix]]** userland command: decompress **gzip-format** files to **standard output** without removing the archive — the usual **`cat(1)`** pattern applied to **`.gz`** data (on typical **GNU** and **BSD** systems, **`zcat`** is the **`gzip`** binary invoked under that name, i.e. **`gzip -dc`** semantics).
	- **Not a standalone project** in practice: shipped as part of **`gzip`** (e.g. [GNU gzip](https://www.gnu.org/software/gzip/)); some systems historically associated the name with **`compress(1)`** **`.Z`** files — see local **`man zcat`**.
