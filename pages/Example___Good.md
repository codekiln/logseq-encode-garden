- # Exemplar / Good Example
	- An **example** is something that *illustrates* a concept.
	  An **exemplar** is something that *represents it at a high or ideal standard*.
	  
	  More concretely:
	- **Example**
		- Purpose: clarify or explain
		- Quality: doesn’t matter much
		- Role: “one instance among many”
		- e.g., a random sentence showing a grammar rule
	- **Exemplar**
		- Purpose: model or benchmark
		- Quality: typically excellent or canonical
		- Role: “this is what it should look like”
		- e.g., a perfectly written essay used as a standard
		  
		  In short:
		  **example = illustrative instance**
		  **exemplar = ideal instance**
