- # CMMC
	- ## Overview
		- **CMMC** stands for **Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification**.
		- It is the Department of Defense (DoD)'s mechanism for validating that defense contractors implement required cybersecurity practices, including those from [[NIST/800-171]].
	- ## Relationship to [[NIST/800-171]]
		- [[NIST/800-171]] defines security requirements for protecting Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) in nonfederal systems.
		- CMMC is the certification and assessment framework used to verify conformance to those requirements for relevant DoD contracts.
