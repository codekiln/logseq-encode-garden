- # [Cachix](https://www.cachix.org/)
	- Hosted [[Nix]] [[Binary/Cache]] service for sharing binaries between CI, development, and deployment environments.
	- Feature shape: push or watch Nix store outputs, configure consumers with `cachix use`, protect read access with auth tokens, and rely on cache hosting features such as garbage collection, access management, CI guides, deduplication against `cache.nixos.org`, and CDN-backed delivery.
	- [Documentation](https://docs.cachix.org/)
