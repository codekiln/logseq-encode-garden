- # Alistair Mavin
	- ## About
		- Independent requirements specialist based in Germany, known internationally for requirements training, coaching, and consulting.
		- Originator of [[EARS]] (Easy Approach to Requirements Syntax), developed with colleagues at Rolls-Royce PLC and first published in 2009.
		- The professional title "Requirements Specialist" is notably uncommon in systems and software engineering — Mavin is one of the rare practitioners who carries it as a primary designation.
