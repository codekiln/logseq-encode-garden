- # [IETF](https://www.ietf.org/)
	- The Internet Engineering Task Force is the premier Internet standards organization: an open global community that develops technical documents and standards for the Internet.
	- The IETF follows open and well-documented processes for setting standards, and published standards are made freely available.
	- Daily work happens through community participation, working groups, mailing lists, Internet-Drafts, RFCs, meetings, hackathons, and the [IETF Datatracker](https://datatracker.ietf.org/).
	- ## Structure
		- [Internet Engineering Steering Group](https://www.ietf.org/about/groups/iesg/) - manages the standards process and technical work.
		- [Internet Architecture Board](https://www.iab.org/) - architectural oversight and liaison role.
		- [Internet Research Task Force](https://irtf.org/) - related research groups.
		- [RFC Editor](https://www.rfc-editor.org/) - publication function for RFCs.
		- [IETF Administration LLC](https://www.ietf.org/about/administration/) - administrative support.
		- [IETF Trust](https://trustee.ietf.org/) - holds IETF-related intellectual property and marks.
