- # Don Norman
	- ## About
		- Cognitive scientist and design researcher. His 1981 *Datamation* article “The truth about Unix: The user interface is horrid” criticized early Unix for cognitive-engineering gaps in end-user comprehension—an influential counterpoint to tool-centric [[Unix/Philosophy]].
