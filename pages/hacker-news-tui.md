- # [hacker-news-tui](https://github.com/danfry1/hacker-news-tui)
	- [[GitHub/Star]] count: 38 as of [[2026-06-27 Sat]].
	- A terminal UI for browsing Hacker News — feeds, threaded comments, and bookmarks. Built with [[Rust]] and [[Ratatui]].
	- A newer and smaller project than [[HN/TUI]] (by [[Person/Thang Pham]], 710 [[GitHub/Star]]s as of [[2026-06-27 Sat]]).
	- [Supply chain security](https://github.com/danfry1/hacker-news-tui#supply-chain-security): no avoidable dependencies — browser opening and HTML decoding are a few lines of `std` in `src/util.rs` rather than extra crates.
