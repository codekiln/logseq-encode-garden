- # Lao Tzu
	- ## About
		- Traditionally credited as the author of [[Book/Tao Te Ching]] and a foundational figure in Daoism.
		- The name means "Old Master"; historical details are uncertain, and the figure is often treated as legendary or semi-legendary in scholarship.
		- Later tradition places him in the Zhou period and makes him a contemporary of Confucius, but those details are not firmly established.
