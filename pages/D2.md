- # [D2](https://d2lang.com/)
	- Declarative **diagram language** and compiler (from **Terrastruct**) that targets **SVG/PNG** with aesthetics and “diagram compiler” ergonomics that often beat plain **[[Diagram/Mermaid]]** for polished output.
	- **Icons and images on nodes** from URLs or local files; Terrastruct hosts **software-architecture-oriented icon sets** suited to mixed stacks (cloud, Git hosts, runtimes, vendors).
	- **Tradeoff**: not the same out-of-the-box experience as Python `from diagrams.aws...` imports in [[diagrams]]—you typically maintain your own **icon/name mapping** for a dense service map.
