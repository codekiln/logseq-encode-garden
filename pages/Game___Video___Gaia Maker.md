- # [Gaia Maker](https://garkimasera.itch.io/gaia-maker)
	- Planet-wide terraforming simulation built with [[Bevy]] in [[Rust]]; reshape barren planets into life-filled worlds, foster animal life, cultivate civilizations—or destroy them.
	- Simulates temperature, biomass, carbon cycle, insolation, and atmospheric composition; facilities include oxygen generators, fusion reactors, and Dyson swarms.
