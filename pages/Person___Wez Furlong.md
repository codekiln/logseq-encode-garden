- # [Wez Furlong](https://wezfurlong.org/)
	- Systems programmer and long-time open source contributor; creator of [[WezTerm]]
	- Has worked at Meta/Facebook on infrastructure and developer tooling
	- Built [[WezTerm]] (2018) as a personal terminal that grew into a widely-used cross-platform emulator; notable for its extensive image protocol support and Lua-based configuration
	- Also created the `wezterm` Rust ecosystem including `wezterm-term` (a terminal model library) and related crates
