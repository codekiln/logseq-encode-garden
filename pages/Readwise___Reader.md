- # [Readwise Reader](https://read.readwise.io/)
	- a paid product from [[Readwise]] that enables one to
		- 1 - read [[PDF]], [[Markdown]], [[HTML]] articles that are saved to the readwise library
		- 2 - serve as an [[RSS/Reader]] and a place to read [[Email/Newsletter]]s
		- 3 - provide the ability to add highlights with plain text comments
		- 4 - provide the ability to save cached versions of documents and republish them with one's highlights displayable alongside the original
