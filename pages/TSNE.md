- # t-distributed stochastic neighbor embedding
	- ## Overview
		- **t-SNE** is a nonlinear technique for visualizing high-dimensional data in two or three dimensions by giving each datapoint a location in a map such that similar objects are modeled by nearby points and dissimilar ones by distant points with high probability.

		- [t-distributed stochastic neighbor embedding - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/T-distributed_stochastic_neighbor_embedding)
