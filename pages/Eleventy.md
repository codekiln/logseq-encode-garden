- # [Eleventy](https://www.11ty.dev/)
	- A simpler static site generator written in JavaScript. Templates can be authored in Markdown, Nunjucks, Liquid, Handlebars, EJS, JSX, and several other formats; no client-side JavaScript is required in the output.
	- Source repository: [11ty/eleventy](https://github.com/11ty/eleventy).
