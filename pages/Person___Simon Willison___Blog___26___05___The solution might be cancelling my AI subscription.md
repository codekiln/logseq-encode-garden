	- Link post by [[Person/Simon Willison]] on [[Person/David Wilson/Blog/26/05/The solution might be cancelling my AI subscription]].
	- ## Highlights
		- "I didn't mean to build most of these things... one hour later the result is not a quick script for X, nor is my problem solved." — [[Person/David Wilson]]
		- "This technology is **horrific** for attention. It's a thermonuclear ADHD amplifier." — [[Person/David Wilson]]
