- # `dotfiles-dep-stage::` — intake stage of a dotfiles dependency
	- The stage of a [[Logseq/Entity/Dotfiles/Dep]] as it moves from first idea to selection. On an instance, set it to one of the permitted value pages below, for example `dotfiles-dep-stage:: [[Logseq/Entity/Dotfiles/Dep/Frontmatter/dotfiles-dep-stage/4 - Vetting]]`.
	- ## Permitted values
		- [[Logseq/Entity/Dotfiles/Dep/Frontmatter/dotfiles-dep-stage/1 - Incubating]]
		- [[Logseq/Entity/Dotfiles/Dep/Frontmatter/dotfiles-dep-stage/2 - Researching]]
		- [[Logseq/Entity/Dotfiles/Dep/Frontmatter/dotfiles-dep-stage/3 - Shortlisted]]
		- [[Logseq/Entity/Dotfiles/Dep/Frontmatter/dotfiles-dep-stage/4 - Vetting]]
		- [[Logseq/Entity/Dotfiles/Dep/Frontmatter/dotfiles-dep-stage/5 - Selected]]
		- [[Logseq/Entity/Dotfiles/Dep/Frontmatter/dotfiles-dep-stage/Adopted]]
		- [[Logseq/Entity/Dotfiles/Dep/Frontmatter/dotfiles-dep-stage/Declined]]
		- [[Logseq/Entity/Dotfiles/Dep/Frontmatter/dotfiles-dep-stage/Retired]]
	- ## Notes
		- The numeric prefix carries the sequence 1→5. `Adopted`, `Declined`, and `Retired` are unnumbered, off-sequence states: `Adopted` is the backlog of dependencies that entered outside this system, `Declined` is a terminal off-ramp reachable from any earlier stage, and `Retired` marks a dependency removed after having been in use.
