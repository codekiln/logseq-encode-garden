- # [MITRE](https://www.mitre.org/)
	- The MITRE Corporation is an American not-for-profit organization that operates federally funded research and development centers (FFRDCs) on behalf of U.S. government agencies, working across defense, cybersecurity, aviation, healthcare, and other public-interest domains.
	- In security it is best known for maintaining widely adopted, freely available reference standards and knowledge bases that the industry conforms to.
	- ## Notable security standards and knowledge bases
		- [[CWE]] - Common Weakness Enumeration, the taxonomy of software and hardware weakness types.
		- [CVE](https://www.cve.org/) - Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures, identifiers for specific disclosed vulnerabilities.
		- [ATT&CK](https://attack.mitre.org/) - adversary tactics and techniques knowledge base.
		- [CAPEC](https://capec.mitre.org/) - Common Attack Pattern Enumeration and Classification.
		- [D3FEND](https://d3fend.mitre.org/) - a knowledge graph of defensive countermeasures.
