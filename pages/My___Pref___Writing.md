- # My Writing Preferences
	- The goal is reader comprehension, not sounding smart. As with [[My/Principle/Favor Readers Over Writers]],  assume the reader is intelligent but does not share your background, and write so they understand it on the first read.
	- This grounds in the [[US/Gov/Plain Writing Act]] of 2010 and the federal plain-language guidance that followed it. Plain language, not a readability score, is the target.
