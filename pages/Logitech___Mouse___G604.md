- Logitech G604 Mouse
	- Pros
		- 8-11 programmable buttons
		- wireless
		- AA batteries (replaceable without changing the mouse)
	- Cons
		- requires spyware, privacy-violating, bug-ridden, unintuitive [[Logitech/GHub]] software
		- exterior rubber and switches degraded within 16 months of mild use
			- auto double-clicking made it unusable
			- this is a well-known issue for this mouse; some enthusiasts on youtube have even made videos of how to replace the switches
