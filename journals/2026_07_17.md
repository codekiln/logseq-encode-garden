# If you can't remember why you installed it, remove it
	- This morning I compared  what was brew installed on one of my machines against what was in [[My/Dotfiles]]'  `chezmoi/Brewfile` and uncovered that many [[brew]] things were installed on my machine but never declared in my brewfile.
	- Wrote [[My/Dotfiles/Process/Adding a New Global Dependency/Idea]]
		- Similar to my dotfiles principle that nothing should be installed without writing down why I need it installed, I am considering have a rule governing my dotfiles that in order to stay installed, I must *remember* why I wanted it installed.
		- I could use [[SRS]] / [[FSRS]] flashcards to determine whether something should be uninstalled. If I failed to to explain why something's installed enough times, then the dependency would get removed from my dotfiles automatically.
