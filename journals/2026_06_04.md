- [[Music]]
	- I've been really enjoying [ear](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ear_(band))'s new album [Rumspringa](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wvCHjGqQRNA). It's got synth, ambient recordings, funky melodies, binaural peeks into virtual realities (e.g. [amelie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Am%C3%A9lie)-ish [ernest hood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neighborhoods_(Ernest_Hood_album)) moments). It's like the mirror image of [diegetic music](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diegetic_music). I hear their music and it tells me a story of their actual reality, rather than being told a fictional story where I hear the music the characters are hearing.
