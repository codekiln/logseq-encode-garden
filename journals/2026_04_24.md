## general audience [[Rec]]s for getting up to date on [[AI]]
	- In addition to just checking out the ChatGPT Image 2 model, here are my current go-to "general audience" recommendations for those that want to get up to date on AI:
		- 1 - check out [[Person/Ethan Mollick/Blog]] [https://www.oneusefulthing.org](https://www.oneusefulthing.org)
		- 2 - [[AI/Daily/Brief]] podcast by [[Nathaniel Whittemore]] ([https://aidailybrief.ai](https://aidailybrief.ai)) which has a whole ecosystem, including training material at [https://aidbtraining.com](https://aidbtraining.com) as well as an "AI Operators" community at [https://aidboperators.circle.so](https://aidboperators.circle.so). In particular, it has great tips for exercises, like the AIDB New Year's challenge ([https://aidbnewyear.com](https://aidbnewyear.com)), which guides people through making a little app that they can use. This is probably the single best thing anyone can do.
