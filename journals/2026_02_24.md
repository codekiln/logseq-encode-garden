- In [[tmux]], the UI would be awesome, if it worked. But in practice, I'm finding that
	- the border between panes isn't thick enough or distinct enough in to make the active pane stand out
	- stdout and stderr in one pane gets displayed in another pane. For example, `npm run dev` in the right hand pane will get displayed in such a way that some of the lines get displayed on the left pane and other lines get displayed on the right pane.
	- parts of the display disappear or flicker, and there's no easy way I've yet found to be able to press a key and get the actual version of the pane to display.
	- as a result, sometimes I'll be in [[Claude Code]] or [[CursorAI/CLI]], and it will ask for my consent for taking various actions, and I won't even be able to see what it's asking me for approval about.
